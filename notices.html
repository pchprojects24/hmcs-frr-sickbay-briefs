Scabies — confirmed case aboard 23 March 2026

One confirmed case of scabies has been identified among ship's company. Scabies is a skin infestation caused by a small mite; it is treatable and not dangerous.

Close contacts (berthmates) may be assessed and treated at the same time. All crew should review the scabies brief for information on how it spreads, what to watch for, and personal hygiene and laundry steps.
