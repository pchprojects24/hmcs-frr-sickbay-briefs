About These Resources

Multiple quit pathways are available to CAF members and Nova Scotia residents. Some programs require a Nova Scotia Health Card; on-base options are available through the Stadacona Pharmacy.

⚕ On-Base Pharmacy Support

CAF members can access nicotine replacement therapy (NRT) directly through the Stadacona base clinic pharmacy.

Pharmacy Tobacco Replacement Therapy Program

Visit the pharmacy at the Stadacona base clinic for assistance with nicotine replacement therapy (NRT).

Pharmacy hours: Monday to Friday, 0730–1530 (closed Thursday 1300–1400).
Inquiries: 721-8757

⚓ Provincial Programs

Free and low-cost provincial quit programs available to Nova Scotia residents.

NSHA — Stop Smoking Program

Group and individual counselling, virtual options, and access to quit aids through Nova Scotia Health Authority.

mha.nshealth.ca/en/stop-smoking-program

Smoke-Free Nova Scotia

Digital resources, counselling referrals, and self-help tools.

smokefreens.ca

Tobacco Free Nova Scotia — Quitline

Quitline, secure chat, SMS support, quit packs, and multilingual resources.

  • Call the Quitline: 811
  • Text SMOKEFREE to +1 902-700-7700
  • Website: tobaccofree.novascotia.ca

✚ PANS — Community Pharmacy Primary Care Clinics

Note: valid Nova Scotia Health Card required

These free cessation services are available at participating PANS pharmacies across Nova Scotia. A valid NS Health Card is required.

Free smoking cessation services including counselling, quit plans, and prescriptions.

Find a Participating Pharmacy

Book online or contact the Pharmacy Association of Nova Scotia:

  • Online booking: pans.ns.ca/quit-or-reduce-smoking-nicotine-products
  • Phone PANS: +1 902-422-9583
