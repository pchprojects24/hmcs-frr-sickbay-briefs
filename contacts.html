Sick Bay — HMCS Frédérick Rolette

For all routine medical and health inquiries, contact Sick Bay through the ship's internal communication system.

  • Location: To be confirmed — check ship's daily orders or ask your divisional staff.
  • Internal extension: To be confirmed by ship's company orders.
  • Routine hours: As posted by the Medical Officer / Medical Technician.

After-hours medical concerns should be directed through the Officer of the Watch.
