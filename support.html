About These Resources

The following programs are available through CAF and provincial health services. All are confidential. Access is voluntary and self-referral is welcomed for most programs.

Canadian Forces Member Assistance Program (CFMAP)

CFMAP is a DND-sponsored employee assistance program offering confidential support to CAF members and their families.

Areas covered include:

  • Marital and family concerns
  • Work-related stress and adjustment
  • Emotional and psychological well-being
  • Financial concerns
  • Support for those affected by sexual assault or harassment
Confidential support line

1-800-268-7708 — available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Mental Health Programs — Stadacona

Four programs are available through Stadacona Medical Clinic's Mental Health Services. All are available to military personnel; some include family members in educational components.

Psychosocial Services

Direct-access counselling for individuals, couples, and families. Self-referral is welcome.

Phone: 902-721-8607

General Mental Health

Assessment, counselling, and follow-up for mental health concerns not related to operational experience.

Phone: 902-721-8012

Operational Trauma & Stress Support Centre

Assessment and treatment for conditions related to operational or occupational exposure, including PTSD.

Phone: 902-721-5703

Addiction Prevention & Treatment

Assessment and treatment for substance use concerns, including individual and group counselling.

Phone: 902-721-8600

For general mental health inquiries or to request a triage appointment: 902-721-8607. Hours: Monday to Friday, 0730–1530.

Chaplaincy

Military chaplains provide pastoral care and counselling to all CAF members regardless of religious affiliation.

  • Support with personal concerns, family stress, and spiritual well-being
  • Counselling around meaning, hope, healing, and life transitions
  • Confidential — not related specifically to religion
  • Self-referral welcome; accessible through the ship or base

Sexual Misconduct Response Centre (SMRC)

The SMRC provides confidential support to anyone who has experienced sexual misconduct in the CAF.

  • Confidential, 24-hour support line: 1-844-750-1648
  • Email: DND.SMRC-CIIS.MDN@forces.gc.ca
  • Support is available regardless of whether a formal complaint is made

Family Support

The Military Family Resource Centre (MFRC) provides programs and services for CAF families throughout the deployment cycle.

  • Counselling and referral services for family members
  • Deployment preparation and reunion support
  • Child and youth programs
  • Individual and group support during absence periods

Contact the Halifax MFRC for specific program information and availability.

Additional Wellness Resources

Provincial Mental Health and Addictions

Nova Scotia provincial line for mental health and addictions information and referrals: 1-888-429-8167

988 — Suicide Prevention & Mental Health Support

A national line for mental health support and suicide prevention (call or text 988). Available 24 hours a day across Canada.
