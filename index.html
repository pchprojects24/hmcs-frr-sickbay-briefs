When to get help (any time)

These red flags mean: stop and see Sick Bay right away, regardless of the specific issue.

Fever with worsening symptoms

Temperature plus spreading redness, confusion, or severe pain.

Spreading infection signs

Rapidly growing redness, red streaks, pus, swelling, or bad smell.

Severe pain or numbness

Intense pain not controlled by basic measures, or loss of feeling/color in a limb.

Can't keep fluids down

Repeated vomiting preventing hydration, or signs of dehydration.

Confusion, fainting, or safety risk

Mental status change, loss of consciousness, or inability to perform duties safely.
